02 SELECTION

FRONTIERS & FOOTPRINTS

“The frontier is not a line, but a space of negotiation.” This year’s curation explores the shifting landscapes of transnational cinema, tracing the physical and emotional journeys that redefine the concept of ‘home’ in a globalised world.

From the rugged terrains of Arizona to the industrial transformations of rural China, we examine how the spirit of the 'lonely knight' persists and evolves across borders.
